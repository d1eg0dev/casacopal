Inquire About Casa Copal

The Destination

Costa Careyes,
Jalisco, Mexico

Founded by Gian Franco Brignone in 1968, Careyes has become a sanctuary for artists, architects, and seekers of an unhurried life. Its colorful casitas, cosmic art installations, and pristine beaches have made it one of the world's best-kept secrets.

The characters you'll meet are as colorful as the place itself — eccentric, elegant, multi-lingual, and always with more than a story to tell.

The House

An architectural poem
written in amber and stone

Casa Copal is a nine-suite private estate perched on the cliffs of Costa Careyes. Its amber-hued walls rise organically from the landscape, crowned by traditional palapa roofs that frame the Pacific like living paintings.

Inside, curved sofas mirror the coastline. Porthole windows echo the nautical spirit of the coast. Every piece of furniture has been crafted by local artisans.
