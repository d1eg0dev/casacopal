The House

Where architecture
surrenders to landscape

Casa Copal is a nine-suite private estate perched on the cliffs of Costa Careyes. Its amber-hued walls rise organically from the landscape, crowned by traditional palapa roofs that frame the Pacific like living paintings.

Inside, curved sofas mirror the coastline. Porthole windows echo the nautical spirit of the coast. Every piece of furniture has been crafted by local artisans — from the teak dining table to the hand-woven ratán lamps.

Common Spaces

Every corner tells
a different story

Living Room

A sculptural space
for gathering

The grand living room is defined by a curving teak-and-linen sofa, a series of porthole windows that track the sun's arc, and curated art pieces. Floor-to-ceiling wood-framed doors open to the terrace, dissolving the line between indoor and outdoor.

Dining & Bar

Where every meal
becomes a celebration

A teppanyaki dining table seats fourteen beneath artisanal ratán pendants. Behind, a fully stocked bar with backlit shelving and premium spirits creates the perfect setting for pre-dinner cocktails and late-night conversations.

Infinity Pool

Where water meets
the horizon

The heated infinity pool stretches toward the Pacific, flanked by teak camastros dressed in white linen and amber-rolled towels bearing the Casa Copal crest. A palapa bar offers cocktails and shade.

Spa & Sauna

Himalayan salt,
Pacific views

A private Finnish sauna with a Himalayan salt wall and panoramic window overlooking the ocean. The ultimate post-surf or pre-dinner ritual.

Fire Pit

Evenings under
Pacific skies

A sunken circular fire pit in amber-hued concrete overlooks the ocean. The perfect setting for sunset mezcals, stargazing, and stories that last until the early hours.

Rooftop Terrace

The highest point,
the widest view

A circular daybed on the rooftop terrace offers 360-degree views of the property, the jungle, and the Pacific coastline. The ideal spot for morning yoga or sunset meditation.

Cinema

Your private
screening room

A dedicated cinema room with premium surround sound, plush seating, and a curated film library. Movie nights with the ocean as your soundtrack.

Dining in Careyes

The Dining List

A curated collection of the finest culinary destinations in Costalegre. From iconic seaside institutions to hidden artistic gems.

Casa de Nada
A celebration of the eclectic and the artistic. This vibrant Mexican cantina offers creative small plates and antojitos paired with a curated selection of spirits. A place for conversation and discovery.
La Coscolina
A charming coastal corner that feels like a Mediterranean escape. Known for artisanal thin-crust pizzas and vibrant salads. Its bohemian atmosphere makes it a favorite for casual gatherings and slow afternoons.
La Duna
Where architectural beauty meets gastronomic innovation. Sophisticated open-air dining with fusion cuisine that balances bold international flavors with local textures. The ideal setting for a sunset dinner overlooking the ocean.
Lilo Beach Club
The epitome of modern coastal living. Fresh, healthy bowls, premium seafood, and tropical mixology served in a setting that defines the new luxury of the Mexican Pacific.
Malabar
The ultimate destination for sophisticated relaxation. Expert mixology and curated bites designed for those who appreciate the art of the cocktail and the beauty of a well-timed aperitivo.
Playa Rosa Beach Club
The heartbeat of the coast. Refined Mediterranean menu enjoyed with your feet in the sand beneath iconic pink architecture. From fresh ceviches to exquisite grilled lobsters — timeless luxury meets simplicity.
Pueblo 25
A sanctuary for the culinary purist. Celebrated for its seasonal farm-to-table ingredients and daily Pacific catches. Mexican heritage reimagined with contemporary finesse.
Shio Sushi
Japanese precision meets Mexican ingredients. Clean, minimalist sushi and sashimi emphasizing the absolute freshness of the local catch. An elegant choice for those seeking a lighter alternative.

Experiences

Careyes unfolds for those
who dare to explore

From the depths of the Pacific to the peaks of the Sierra Madre, Casa Copal is your gateway to the most extraordinary experiences Costalegre has to offer.

Ocean & Coast

Private boat excursions along hidden bays. Snorkeling, surfing, deep-sea fishing, and whale watching in season. Kayaking and paddleboarding from private access.

Wellness & Spirit

Private sauna with Himalayan salt wall. Sound baths at Copa del Sol. Beachside yoga at dawn. Personalized massage and spa treatments by the pool.

Culture & Land

Horseback riding on pristine beaches. Polo matches at Careyes Club. Turtle sanctuary visits during nesting season. Art walks and cosmic installations.

Signature Experiences

The best of Careyes

Copa del Sol
Careyes' iconic open-air amphitheater. Attend sunset sound baths, live music performances, and cultural gatherings in one of Mexico's most dramatic natural settings.
Polo at Careyes
Watch world-class polo matches at the Careyes Club, or take introductory lessons on horseback. A signature experience that defines the spirit of the coast.
Turtle Sanctuary
During nesting season, participate in the release of baby sea turtles on Careyes' protected beaches. A transformative experience for guests of all ages.
Art & Architecture
Explore Careyes' cosmic art installations, colorful casitas designed by world-renowned architects, and private collections that dot the coastline. A walking gallery in nature.
Private Excursions
From yacht charters along the Costalegre to guided jungle hikes and visits to artisan villages in the Sierra Madre. Our concierge curates every adventure to your interests.

The Destination

Costa Careyes,
Jalisco, Mexico

Careyes

Where the jungle
meets the sea

Founded by Gian Franco Brignone in 1968, Costa Careyes has become a sanctuary for artists, architects, and seekers of an unhurried life. Its colorful casitas, cosmic art installations, and pristine beaches have made it one of the world's best-kept secrets.

The characters you'll meet are as colorful as the place itself — eccentric, elegant, multi-lingual, and always with more than a story to tell.
